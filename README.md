# 🚲 Smart City Bike Sharing Analysis (Power BI Project)

## 📌 Overview

This project presents a **Power BI dashboard** for analyzing smart city bike-sharing data across multiple cities. It provides insights into bike availability, station status, utilization trends, and geographic distribution to support data-driven urban mobility decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Monitor bike availability across cities
* Analyze station operational status (Open vs Closed)
* Track bike usage trends over time
* Evaluate city-wise utilization and demand
* Visualize geographic distribution of bike stations

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Power BI Desktop
* Data Modeling
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Data Visualization
* Interactive Filters & Slicers

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

* 📍 **City-wise Bike Availability Analysis**
* 🚦 **Station Status Overview (Open vs Closed)**
* 📈 **Trend of Available Bikes Over Time**
* 📊 **Bike Utilization vs Free Stands Comparison**
* 🗺️ **Interactive Map of Bike Stations**
* 📋 **Detailed Station-Level Data Table**

---

## 📈 Key Metrics

* **Total Stations:** 1028
* **Available Bikes:** ~20,000
* **Free Bike Stands:** ~32,000
* **Bike Availability Rate:** 34.34%
* **Operational Stations:** ~87% Open

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* Majority of stations are operational (~87%)
* Some cities show higher bike demand and lower availability
* Bike availability fluctuates over time, indicating usage patterns
* Certain stations frequently have zero availability, suggesting high demand
* Geographic distribution helps identify high-density service areas
